Normal,
Cataract,

4.

B. Disease_Risk: Presence of disease/abnormality.
C. DR: Presence of diabetic retinopathy.
D. ARMD: Presence of age-related macular degeneration.
E. MH: Presence of media haze.
F. DN: Presence of drusen.
G. MYA: Presence of myopia.
H. BRVO: Presence of branch retinal vein occlusion.
I. TSLN: Presence of tessellation
